Dev Bod

part 1: surviving an office workstation

Be human shaped
- if you are bent or curved, your risk of injury is much higher
- use a monitor at your eye level
- use wrist rests to keep wrists from being bent
- avoid naked laptop use!

Sitting is about as bad for you as smoking
- https://www.acpjournals.org/doi/abs/10.7326/M14-1651
- Use a standing desk or desk adapter
- take short walks to think and get away from the desk
- incorporate some sort of core exercise and stretches / twists into daily routine

Pay attention to warning signs
- avoiding eyestrain - look at something far away a few times an hour
- wrist pain
- finger numbness
- back pain
